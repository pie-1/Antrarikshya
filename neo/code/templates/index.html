Near-Earth Objects (NEOs)

What are Near-Earth Objects?

Near-Earth Objects (NEOs) are asteroids or comets that come close to Earth's orbit. They range in size from small rocks to large celestial bodies that can pose significant threats to Earth. NASA and other space organizations keep track of these objects to predict any potential impacts.
